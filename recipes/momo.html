Momo Recipe

What are Momos?

Momos are dumplings made with flour and a savory stuffing. This stuffing can be made with veggies, sea-food, chicken or paneer

List of Ingredients :

  • All-purpose flour
  • Oil
  • Salt
  • Water
  • Spring Onions
  • Chopped Garlic
  • Preferred Filling

Steps:

  1. Making the dough
  2. Making the stuffing
  3. Shape the dough
  4. Steam the Momos
